What I intended to be a short walk turned into a three hour adventure.<br>
I'm not sure if I discovered any new places, but I saw some beautiful flowers (as well as some fern), and it allowed me to use my 1.8f lens some more.<br>
On my way home near the train station of teh village I passed though a beautiful black cat was sitting in the shade.
I crouched down, hoping to be able to take a couple of quick pictures, but it actually started walking toward me.
I think it got a bit startled by the shutter sound though, since it didn't get too close and then walked away.<br>
And I could've brought the lens hood so prevent the purple-blueish mist on the cat's body, but eh, I don't mind it too much.
